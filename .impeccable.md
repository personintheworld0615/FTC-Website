## Design Context

### Users
- **Target Audience**: Parents, students, mentors, school administrators, and potential sponsors.
- **Job to be Done**: Learn about FTC Team 19772, register for sponsorship, check team members, access resources, and feel inspired to support or join the team.
- **Emotional Goals**: Professionalism and high-tech capability blended with community warmth, accessibility, and high-performance engineering.

### Brand Personality
- **Voice & Tone**: High-performance, friendly, strong, premium.
- **Visual Vibe**: Apple-meets-Stripe: clean layouts, elegant type hierarchy, warm brand tones, and flawless interactive details.
- **Theme**: Warm Light Mode only. No dark mode options. Tinted neutrals with a vibrant orange/terracotta brand color.
- **Anti-References**: Avoid neon sci-fi, chaotic/over-decorated grids, or overly low-key/boring layouts.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Typography**: Refined, tight-tracking sans-serif headings paired with a premium serif for dramatic accents (Apple/Stripe editorial style).
- **Color Palette**: Warm off-white backgrounds (`oklch(0.99 0.01 85)`), deep chocolate/charcoal text (`oklch(0.15 0.02 25)`), and energetic orange/terracotta accents.
- **Motion & Interactions**: Premium and polished (magnetic buttons, smooth height transitions, subtle physics). Engaging micro-animations like an SVG robot traversing the screen or scanning indicators, but kept under control.

### Design Principles
1. **Premium Warmth**: Every page must use a warm light-mode aesthetic that feels inviting to the community yet extremely professional to sponsors.
2. **Intentional Motion**: Use high-end, smooth animations (like a robot moving from left to right or entering elements) that delight the user without causing visual fatigue.
3. **Structured Polish**: Emulate Apple and Stripe: generous white space, clear horizontal alignment, asymmetric layouts, and crisp card borders (no side-stripe indicators).
